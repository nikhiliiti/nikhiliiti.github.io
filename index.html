I am currently working as an Assistant Professor at Indian Institute of Information Technology Sri City. Prior to my current role, I was working as a postdoctoral security researcher at Fraunhofer SIT in close collaboration with TU Darmstadt, Germany. I earned the Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Indian Institute of Technology Indore, India, in 2019.


My broad area of interests are Internet Security, System Security, and Computer Networks. In particular, I work on:

- Vulnerability Assessment of Application Layer Protocols

- Next generation DoS/DDoS attacks

- Devising Real-time Cyber Threat Mitigation Strategies



I am looking for motivated students willing to pursue research in the cybersecurity domain. Interested candidates may send me their CVs.
