Nikhil Tripathi

I am a network security researcher at Fraunhofer SIT (TU Darmstadt), Germany. I obtained my PhD in Computer Science and Engineering in 2019 from Indian Institute of Technology Indore in India. I received my Master of Technology (M. Tech.) in Information Technology in 2014 from University of Hyderabad in India. My research is focused on the identifying vulnerabilities in popular network protocols and proposing appropriate defense mechanisms. Here is a link to my CV.
